Do justice, love kindness, and walk humbly. According to the book of Micah, that is what is required of us on this earthly journey. This fall, join fellow pilgrims in a physical exploration of this verse. We will hike humbly through the scenic Arkansas woods, camping along the way, learning from the natural world and one another. Follow the path and discover where justice, kindness, and humility intersect.

The journey will begin at Christ Church in Little Rock, where we will attend the All Saints’ service on Thursday evening, share a meal, sort gear, and get to know one another. We will camp at the church and then caravan to the trailhead early Friday morning.

For three days we will follow Eagle Rock Loop, winding twenty-six miles through one of the most remote sections of the Ouachita National Forest. The scenery is breathtaking but the hiking can be too. The loop guides hikers over mountaintops, across rivers, and through the Winding Stairs area where there are caves and cliffs to be explored.

On Sunday morning we will celebrate the Eucharst high on Eagle Rock, overlooking the Ouachita Mountains, then complete the loop and head back to Christ Church, arriving that evening.

“The spiritual life, in my experience, is a matter of moving ever deeper into backcountry, both literally and figuratively.” - Belden Lane, from Backpacking With the Saints

The trip will be led by Ragan Sutterfield, James Matthews, and Jason Alexander, Episcopalians and outdoor enthusiasts. Together they bring backcountry experience, training in wilderness emergency response, and a passion for exploring the spiritual life.

Space is limited to twelve participants, each of whom will need to feel comfortable hiking eight miles per day with a pack. Participants will also need to bring, at minimum, a backpack, sleeping bag, and hiking shoes. The cost for the trip is $75 per participant, which will cover the cost of evening common meals and transportation expenses.

Please contact us if you are interested. We look forward to sharing this experience with you!
